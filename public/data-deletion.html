Data Deletion Request

Last updated: 2025

At MyDoctor, users may request full deletion of their account and all related data.

How to Request Data Deletion

Email us at:

support@mydoctor-app.com

Include the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number or email used in the app
  • Subject: "MyDoctor Data Deletion Request"

What Will Be Deleted?

Upon request, we will delete:

  • Your user account
  • Profile details
  • Appointment history
  • Any optional data you provided

Clinic listings imported from public sources (Google Places & Facebook Pages) cannot be deleted because they are public, not user-created.

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are processed within 48–72 hours.
